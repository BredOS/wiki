# 1. Introduction
The Orion O6 Family is a series of boards produced by Radxa and based on the Cix P1 CD8180 SoC. Boards in this family support booting via UEFI, providing users with the ability to boot any kernel that supports ACPI.

# 2. Download
You can find download links for the aarch64 iso on our [Github page](https://github.com/BredOS/bredos-iso/releases/latest)!

We have two versions available: one is based on Radxa's 6.6 kernel, and the other is based on mainline.
